How to make a tea?

Tea is one of the most popular hot beverage in the world. It is especially more famous in Asia and Uk. People prefer to drink a cup of tea in the morning for a fresh start. It can be prepared with milk or without milk. Today in this recipe we will learn to prepare it with milk

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk (250 ml)
  • 2 Teaspoon Tea Powder
  • 1/4 cup of water (60 ml)
  • 3 Teaspoon sugar

Steps :

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add sugar and tea powder in it and boil it for 3-4 minutes on medium flame.
  3. Add milk and boil it over medium flame for 6-7 minutes or until bubble starts to rise. You will see the change in color of the tea from milky shade to brown shade when it is ready.
  4. Turn off the gas and strain tea in cups.
