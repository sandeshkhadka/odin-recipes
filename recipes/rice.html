How to cook Rice?

Rice is a famous food items in Asia. People eat rice as lunch and dinner generally. It is best served with dhal and curry. There are lot of methods to cook rice but today we will discuss about cooking it in a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • Rice (1cup)
  • Water (3 cup)

Steps:

  1. Rinse the rice.
  2. Put the rice and water in Rice Cooker.
  3. Cook it for 3 whistle.
  4. Your rice is ready.
